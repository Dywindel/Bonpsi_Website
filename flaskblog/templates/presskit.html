SNAPto - A Sokoban-Snapping Puzzle Game

SNAPto is a sokobon-style puzzle game based around a snapping mechanic. Players are given a series of dense and deceptively simple puzzles with incrementally increasing difficulty. Each puzzle is unique in introducing a principle of the game's core mechanics and each build upon each other. The player is asked to discover the rules and story of this world through experimentation, but is never certain what that entails.

The game is now available to play on itch.io.

The game is designed by Luke Baker, a research associate working in glasgow. This work is inspired by Stephen Lavelle's game; Stephen's Sausage Roll.
